# Sprint 1: Custom License Exploration and Pull Mechanism

## Goal
Explore the drafting of a custom "Animus License" that combines the copyleft properties of GPL 3.0 with the specific patent/trademark protections of Apache 2.0. Decide between this custom license and the current Dual License. Once decided, implement the cross-repository pull mechanism to ensure all Animus Ecosystem projects are properly licensed.
